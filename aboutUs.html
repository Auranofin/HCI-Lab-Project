About CAteriNgz

CAteriNgz is a premier catering service delivering exceptional culinary experiences for events in Jakarta, Bandung, and Malang. Originating from a small kitchen, we now offer authentic Indonesian flavors using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients. Our commitment to sustainability ensures eco-friendly practices in all our operations. We collaborate with local farmers and artisans to bring you the best of Indonesian cuisine, from savory rendang to delightful satay. Whether for family gatherings or corporate events, CAteriNgz guarantees a memorable culinary journey with each bite, making every occasion truly special.

Our Story

CAteriNgz's Story

In the bustling streets of Jakarta, in 1999, Sari, a young woman with a deep-rooted love for Indonesian culinary traditions, decided to turn her passion into a reality. Raised in a family where cooking was more than just a daily chore but a sacred ritual, Sari learned the art of blending spices, crafting sambals, and cooking with fresh, locally sourced ingredients from her mother and grandmother. Every dish told a story, rich with cultural heritage and flavors that had been passed down through generations.

Sari's journey began in a modest kitchen of her family home, where the walls were lined with heirloom recipes and the air was thick with the aroma of traditional dishes like rendang, nasi goreng, and sate ayam. Her weekends were filled with laughter and shared meals, where friends and family would gather to enjoy her culinary creations. It was during one of these cherished gatherings that a family friend, enamored by her food, suggested she share her talent with a broader audience.

Embracing this idea, Sari began offering her culinary services for small community events and local gatherings. The response was overwhelmingly positive, as people were drawn to the authentic flavors and the comforting sense of nostalgia each bite offered. Encouraged by this feedback, Sari decided to formalize her venture, and thus CAteriNgz was born.

Initially, Sari operated out of her family kitchen, cooking with love and delivering meals in her small car. Her menu was a celebration of Indonesian culture, featuring dishes that highlighted the diverse flavors of the archipelago. From the spicy richness of West Sumatran rendang to the sweet and savory delights of Central Javanese gudeg, each dish was a homage to Indonesia's culinary diversity.

As word spread about CAteriNgz, the demand grew beyond Jakarta, reaching the cultural hubs of Bandung and the scenic city of Malang. Sari's commitment to quality and her use of locally sourced, seasonal ingredients resonated with her clients, and her reputation for excellence was solidified.

Over the years, CAteriNgz has grown from a small home-based business to a respected name in the catering industry. Sari now operates a dedicated commercial kitchen, working alongside a team of skilled chefs who share her passion for Indonesian cuisine. Each dish they create is not just food but a cultural experience, a connection to the rich culinary heritage of Indonesia.

Today, CAteriNgz is known for bringing a taste of home to every event they cater, from family reunions to grand weddings, and corporate events. Sari's journey, which started in a humble kitchen, has blossomed into a thriving business that celebrates the rich cultural tapestry of Indonesian cuisine, ensuring that the flavors of her heritage continue to delight and inspire people across the country.

Commitment to Quality

At CAteriNgz, quality is at the heart of everything we do. We work closely with local farmers and artisans to source the freshest ingredients, ensuring that our food not only tastes great but also supports sustainable farming practices. Our chefs take pride in crafting dishes that are not only delicious but also good for the community and the environment.
